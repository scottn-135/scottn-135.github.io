Welcome to Scott's Restaurant

Situated in the busy heart of Melbourne, Scott's Restaurant has been a mainstay in the Melbourne community for the past 10 years, serving up some of the finest in both local and international cuisine.

With it's spacious and family-friendly indoor and outdoor eating areas, licensed bar and weekly live music acts, you can be sure that your experience will be an enjoyable one.
